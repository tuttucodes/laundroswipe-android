Privacy Policy

Effective date: 13 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how LaundroSwipe (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and app at laundroswipe.com.

1. Information we collect

When you use LaundroSwipe, we may collect:

  • Account details: name, phone number, WhatsApp number, email address, college details and hostel / room.
  • Order details: pickup dates, services selected, token number, instructions and order history.
  • Login data: Google sign-in profile information (name, email, profile ID) if you choose to use Google.
  • Technical data: device type, browser type and basic usage logs for security and troubleshooting.

2. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • create and manage your LaundroSwipe account;
  • schedule pickups, assign tokens and update order status;
  • send essential notifications about your orders;
  • improve our product and customer support; and
  • comply with legal or regulatory obligations where applicable.

3. How your data is stored

LaundroSwipe uses Supabase (hosted Postgres database and authentication service) to store user and order data. Your account and orders are stored securely in Supabase, and access is restricted through role-based and row-level security policies.

4. Sharing of information

We do not sell your data. We only share information with:

  • Service vendors who handle your laundry (they see your token, name and order details necessary to provide the service).
  • Service providers like Supabase and hosting providers (Vercel) that power the app.
  • Authorities if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

5. Cookies and tracking

LaundroSwipe currently uses minimal client-side storage (for example, to keep you signed in) and standard server logs. If we introduce analytics or additional tracking, we will update this Policy and, where required, ask for your consent.

6. Data retention

We keep your account and order data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. We may retain certain information for longer if required for legal, accounting, or legitimate business purposes.

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • access the personal information we hold about you;
  • request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information;
  • request deletion of certain data, subject to legal limitations; and
  • withdraw consent for optional communications.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details in the Contact us section below.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, but no system can be completely secure. Please keep your login details safe and do not share your token number publicly.

9. Third-party links

LaundroSwipe may link to third-party sites (for example, payment or support tools). Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, not this one.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective date” at the top will change when we do. Continued use of LaundroSwipe after changes means you accept the updated Policy.
